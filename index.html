About

A studio built around craft.

Pixelcraft is an independent app development studio focused on the Apple ecosystem. We design and build native iOS applications end to end — from product direction and interface design through engineering, testing, and release. Our approach pairs careful, human-centered design with modern, maintainable engineering.

We believe good software is quiet and dependable: it respects your time, protects your data, and does exactly what it promises. Every product we ship is held to that standard.

Design-led

Interfaces that feel native to iOS — clear, considered, and comfortable to use every day.

Engineered to last

Native Swift apps built for performance, reliability, and long-term maintainability.

Privacy-minded

We collect only what an app needs to work, and we're transparent about how it's used.

Independent

A focused team shipping our own products, with full ownership of the details.

Styled

Styled is a wardrobe management and personal styling app for iPhone. It helps you catalogue the clothes you already own, put together outfits with confidence, and get more out of your wardrobe.

  • Catalogue the clothes you already own
  • Plan, build, and save outfits
  • Make the most of your wardrobe
  • Designed for iPhone, built with privacy in mind
